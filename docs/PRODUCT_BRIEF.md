# Foundry

## Vision

Foundry is a creative workspace that helps designers, developers, marketers, and creative teams organize ideas before execution.

Instead of juggling notes, screenshots, inspiration, files, and project briefs across multiple tools, Foundry brings everything together into one focused workspace.

---

## Problem

Creative projects often begin with information scattered across emails, cloud storage, Pinterest boards, screenshots, documents, and notes.

Foundry provides a single place to collect those pieces before design or development begins.
